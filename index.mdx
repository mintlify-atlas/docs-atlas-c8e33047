## What is Gamdl?

Gamdl (Glomatico's Apple Music Downloader) is an open-source command-line tool that lets you download songs, music videos, and post videos from Apple Music. It supports a wide range of audio codecs — from standard AAC to lossless ALAC and Dolby Atmos — and can download music videos at resolutions up to 4K.

Gamdl automatically tags downloaded files with comprehensive metadata (album art, artist, track number, lyrics, and more) and gives you full control over file naming and organization through customizable templates.

## Supported URL types

Gamdl accepts Apple Music URLs for the following content types:

- Songs
- Albums (public and library)
- Playlists (public and library)
- Music videos
- Artists
- Post videos
- Apple Music Classical

## Requirements

Before using Gamdl, you need:

- **Python 3.10 or higher**
- **An active Apple Music subscription**
- **Browser cookies** exported in Netscape format from the Apple Music website

<Note>
  Gamdl requires valid Apple Music cookies from a logged-in account with an active subscription. Without a subscription, downloads will not work.
</Note>
